Black

01 / 10

Black is the darkest color, the result of the absence of all visible light. Throughout history, Black has symbolized power, elegance, and mystery. In fashion, Black is timeless — a little Black dress never goes out of style. In art, Black is used to create depth and contrast, giving life to every other color around it. Scientists tell us that Black absorbs all wavelengths of light, reflecting none back to our eyes. In nature, Black appears in the darkest night sky, in the deepest oceans, and in the wings of a raven. Culturally, Black represents both mourning and sophistication. In technology, Black screens and devices feel premium and sleek. Truly, Black is not just a color — it is a feeling, a statement, and a universe of its own.
